Scrambled Eggs

The best way to start your day are some Scrambled Eggs!

This dish consist of protein-packed foods such as beans, eggs and cheese.

This food goal is to hit many macros such as carbs with the rice, fats with the cheese, and protein with beans, eggs and maybe some chicken breast

Ingredients:

  • 300g of rice
  • 4 large eggs
  • 50g of cheese
  • 100g of cooked beans
  • 150g of cooked chicken breast
