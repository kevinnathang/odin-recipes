Scrambled Eggs

The best way to start your day are some Scrambled Eggs!

This dish consist of protein-packed foods such as beans, eggs and cheese.

This food goal is to hit many macros such as carbs with the rice, fats with the cheese, and protein with beans, eggs and maybe some chicken breast

Ingredients:

  • 300g of rice
  • 4 large eggs
  • 50g of cheese
  • 100g of cooked beans
  • 150g of cooked chicken breast

Step by step:

  1. Scramble the eggs on a pan until they start to get more consistent
  2. Add in the rice, beans and cheese
  3. Add in any favorite dressing you may want to use such as sesame or shoyu
  4. Place the mixture on a bowl and top it up with the chicken breast
  5. Enjoy!
